## 1. Scientific context

Markets exhibit **volatility regime changes**, contagion and asset synchronization. They are not “living systems” biologically, but they share **coupling transitions** in multivariate series.

## 2. Why the classical panel is not enough

- Realized vol, VIX, rolling return correlations are often **reactive**.
- Regime ML (HMM, etc.) can predict without offering ordinal Φ₁–Φ₃-style decomposition.
