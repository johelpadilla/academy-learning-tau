### Learning outcomes
1. Extend cardio (RR) intuition to a sleep triad: HRV · activity · temperature.
2. Observe a driver change (clean circadian → high-frequency fragmentation).
3. Compare maturity: CCTP is the anchor; sleep is a physiological bridge.

**Maturity:** ★★★☆☆ — Synthetic demo aligned with sleep-architecture ideas.

## 1. Scientific context

Sleep architecture coordinates autonomic and behavioural variables. When the circadian driver fragments, it is not only “more noise”: **ordinal relations** among HRV, activity and temperature reorganize.
