# Domain: Social dynamics — polarization (toy model)

### Learning outcomes
1. See a polarization cascade as ordinal reorganization of opinions + interaction.
2. Separate pedagogical method use from any social/political prediction claim.
3. Contrast with AR control: without shared latent, |Δτ_s| is usually small.

**Maturity:** ★★☆☆☆ — Conceptual demo. No validated social cohort on the platform.

---

## 1. Scientific context

In simple opinion models, two poles and interaction intensity can pass from a “noisy pluralist” regime to an **anti-correlated** high-interaction regime. That is a change of **relational structure**, not only of the mean of a polarization indicator.

---

## 2. Why the classical panel is not enough

- No real social-network data in v1.0.
- No claim to predict elections, conflict or virality.
- Demo exists to **teach the grammar** of τ_s / RECD in collective systems.

---

## 3. Value of τ_s + RECD

- Variables: `opinion_a`, `opinion_b`, `interaction`.
- Post-event: polarization latent forces A ≈ −B and inflates interaction.
- Dual reading asks whether Δ is residual vs cross-independence surrogates.
